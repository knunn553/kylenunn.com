2017-2018 Season

Chattanooga State

  • Made 13 appearances, including six starts, and posted a 2-5 record with a 4.18 ERA in first season at Chattanooga State.
  • Struck out 64 batters in 56 innings (10.29 K/9) and held opponents to a .218 batting average.
  • Posted nine strikeouts over seven scoreless innings in victory against Roane State, March 10.
  • Notched first win at Chattanooga State with 4.2 innings of relief at West Georgia Tech, Feb. 17.

2018-2019 Season

Chattanooga State

  • Appeared in nine games, started twice and finished with a 3-2 record and 4.59 ERA in second season at Chattanooga State Community College.
  • Recorded 38 strikeouts in 33.1 innings pitched (10.26 K/9) and held opponents to a .208 batting average.
  • Won final start at Chattanooga State, striking out five in 5.2 innings against Columbia State, May 6.
  • Struck out eight batters in 6.1-inning start at Cleveland State, March 22.
  • Tossed 5.2 scoreless innings, allowing one hit while striking out six as part of combined shutout against Roane State, March 16.

2019-2020 Season

Austin Peay State University

  • Made five appearances on the mound, including a pair of weekend starts.
  • Began season out of the bullpen, opening APSU career with four-inning effort against Eastern Michigan, Feb. 16, allowing two runs en route to the victory.
  • Tossed two scoreless innings of relief against Samford, Feb. 21, working around three hits.
  • Made first start at Jacksonville, March 1, and went 1.2 innings but followed that with 3.1-inning start against Murray State.

2020-2021 Season

Austin Peay State University

  • Tied for team lead with 19 relief appearances, primarily in middle relief and did not earn a decision during the season.
  • Compiled a 3.52 ERA, struck out 12 batters and held opponents to a .232 batting average in 23.0 innings of work.
  • Did not allow an earned run in 14 of his 19 appearances.
  • Notched an impressive 1.74 ERA in 10 OVC relief outings.

2021-2022 Season

Austin Peay State University

  • Made 19 appearances, finishing the season with a save and 17 strikeouts in 24.0 innings pitched.
  • After allowing three runs in his season’s first appearance, would allow just two runs in his next nine outings which totaled 7.0 innings.
  • Notched his season’s lone save by retiring the final batter of the game against Murray State (May 6).
  • Made season’s longest appearance against Morehead State (May 19), tossing 4.2 innings of scoreless relief and striking out a season-high five batters.
